Blueberry Buckle

Makes 8 servings

Select your unit of measurements

Crumble

  • granulated sugar
  • dark brown sugar
  • all-purpose flour
  • ground cinnamon
  • butter, cold

Batter

  • butter
  • granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • flour
  • baking soda
  • buttermilk

Filling

  • blueberries
  • fresh lemon juice
  • flour
  • granulated sugar

Directions

To prepare the crumble, combine all ingredients in a large bowl and break apart with fingers. Refrigerate prepared crumble.

To prepare the batter, cream butter and sugar in a medium-size bowl, then mix in egg. Sift together dry ingredients, then mix in half the dry ingredients to the creamed butter, then mix in buttermilk as well. Add the remaining half of dry ingredients into mixture until just combined. Refrigerate batter for 30 minutes.

To prepare the filling, combine all ingredients in a seperate bowl and sit aside.

Preheat oven to . Grease eight ramekins, muffin pans or one large Pyrex pie pan. Add of batter to each mold smoothing mixture with a plastic spatula. Add of blueberries on top of batter then top with of crumble making sure the crumble is broken up first before topping.

Bake in oven for 30 to 35 minutes until the top is brown and filling just starts to bubble. Remove from oven and allow to cool for an additional 20 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.
